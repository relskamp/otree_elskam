The study will take approximately 90 minutes to complete.

The study is comprised of three parts. Part 1 consists of instructions of how to play the game. At the end of Part 1 we will ask you to fill out a short quiz to test you on how well you understood these instructions.

In Part 2 you will play 25 auction rounds.

Part 3 consists of a short demographic questionnaire.
