In this example, Bidder A earned 2 units while Bidder B and Bidder C earned 3 units. Bidder A’s profits are calculated as

  • Unit 1: $63 - $58 = $5
  • Unit 2: $60 - $52 = $8
  • and nothing on unit 3
  • Total profits = $13

Bidder B’s profits are calculated as

  • Unit 1: $56 - $50 = $6
  • Unit 2: $54 - $42 = $12
  • Unit 3: $41 - $32 = $9
  • Total profits = $27

Bidder C’s profits are calculated as

  • Unit 1: $59 - $51 = $8
  • Unit 2: $57 - $45 = $12
  • Unit 3: $33 - $30 = $3
  • Total profits = $23
